Exercise 1: Media

We can enhance our websites greatly by adding new types of media. One of neat features we've learned how to add to our websites is the audio element. This will let us add sound files to our website so that our users can listen to music. Remember to be judicious when you add media elements with sound, since websites with soundtracks can be jarring to some users.
